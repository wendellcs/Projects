How to play

  • (1) - You can guess one letter at a time.

  • (2) - If a guessed letter is in the word, the letter will fills the corresponding blanks spaces.

  • (3) - If a guessed letter is not in the word, a part of the hangman will be drawn.

  • (4) - You can continue guessing until you either correctly guess the word or the hangman is fully drawn.
